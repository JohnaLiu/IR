Abstract    :
              Support is provided for three educational workshops to be held at the Society of
              Personality and Social Psychology's (SPSP) First Annual Meeting in Nashville,
              TN, February 6, 2000. The workshops include (1) Capturing the Vicissitudes of
              Life: Electronic Experience-Sampling; (2) Functional Neuroimaging in
              Personality and Social Psychology; and (3) Using the Web for Research in Social
              and Personality Psychology. The workshops will provide an interdisciplinary
              focus on the use of cutting-edge technologies in social and personality
              psychology research.
