Abstract    :
              The Interdisciplinary Conference on Electrical, Electronics & Computer
              Engineering Education in the Third Millennium (ICEECEE) will be held September
              10-15, 2000 in Davos, Switzerland.  ICEECEE is sponsored by the United
              Engineering Foundation and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics
              Engineers (IEEE).  The aim of this conference is to create an opportunity for
              worldwide cooperation in the goals, curricula and methods of Engineering
              Education.

The ICEECEE will fund travel expenses and registration fee for
              young investigators and educators
