This award to University of Texas at Austin provides shipboard
              technical support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument
              calibration for researchers using R/V Longhorn, a research vessel operated by
              University of Texas Marine Science Institute as part of the University-National
              Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet.  The technical support awarded
              here will assist NSF-funded ocean researchers conduct  oceanographic studies in
              the Gulf of Mexico in CY2000.
