This award to San Jose State University provides shipboard
              technical support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument
              calibration for researchers using R/V Point Sur, a research vessel operated by
              Moss Landing Marine Laboratories as part of the University-National
              Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet.  The technical support awarded
              here will assist NSF-funded researchers conduct oceanographic studies in the
              waters along the central California margin and adjacent regions in CY2000 and
              beyond.
