This award to University of Michigan provides shipboard technical
              support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument calibration
              for researchers using R/V Laurentian, a research vessel operated by University
              of Michigan as part of the University-National Oceanographic Laboratory System
              research fleet.  The technical support awarded here will assist NSF-funded
              researchers conduct lacustrine studies in Lake Michigan and the other Great
              Lakes in CY2000 and beyond.
