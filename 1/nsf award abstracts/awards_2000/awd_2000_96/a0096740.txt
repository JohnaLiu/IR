Abstract    :
              The Principle Investigators of this proposal propose to organize a PI meeting
              for the Networking Research Programs (Networking Research Program and Special
              Projects in Networking) in the ANIR Division of NSF.  The PI meeting will be
              held over a two-day period in addition to an opening night reception.  The
              reception will be Wednesday evening, November 1, 2000 with two full days of
              technical sessions, Thursday, November 2, and Friday, November 3.  The PI
              meeting will facilitate the exchange of research ideas among the PIs with
              active NSF awards from the Networking Research Programs and NSF program
              directors.
