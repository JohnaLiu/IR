This award to University of Alaska Fairbanks provides shipboard
              technical support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument
              calibration for researchers using R/V Alpha Helix, a research vessel operated
              by the university's Seward Marine Center as part of the University-National
              Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet.  The technical support awarded
              here will assist NSF-funded researchers conduct oceanographic studies in the
              Gulf of Alaska, Bering Sea and adjacent regions in CY2000 and beyond.
