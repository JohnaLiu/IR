This award to Skidaway Institute of Oceanography in Savannah,
              Georgia provides shipboard technical support, shore-based support, instrument
              maintenance and instrument calibration for researchers using R/V Blue Fin, a
              research vessel operated by University of Georgia System's Skidaway Institute
              of Oceanography as part of the University-National Oceanographic Laboratory
              System research fleet.  The technical support awarded here will assist
              NSF-funded researchers conduct oceanographic studies in the waters along the
              southeast coast of the United States in CY2000 and beyond.
