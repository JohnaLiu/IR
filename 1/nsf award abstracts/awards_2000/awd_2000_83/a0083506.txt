Abstract    :
              Summary:  This research explores the use of geochemical tracers in a marine
              gastropod as an indicator of Chilean coastal upwelling.  Mg/Ca will be used to
              indicate water temperature and Cd/Ca will be used to indicate nutrient content.
               Measurements from a series of water samples will be compared to analyses of
              living shells to test and calibrate the usefulness of the method.
