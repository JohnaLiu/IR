Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of ANIR's
              "Internet Technologies " announcement, NSF 98-104. It provides support for two
              years to address tools and methodologies for network protocol interoperability
              testing, with special emphasis on multicast protocols. Collaborators include
              but are not limited to Cisco Systems, 3COM, and Nortel Networks.
