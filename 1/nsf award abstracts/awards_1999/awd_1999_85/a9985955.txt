Abstract    :
              This award is made under the high performance connections portion of ANIR's
              "Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 98-102. It provides partial
              support for two years for a DS-3 connection for Idaho State University.
              Applications include GIS TreC, remote operation of instrumentation, remote
              sensing imagery, protein characterization, 3-D modeling and others.
              Collaborating institutions include but are not limited to Washington State
              University, University of Idaho, Boise State University , University of
              Montana, Utah State University, MIT, Sandia National Laboratory, and DOE's
              Pacific Northwest National Laboratory.
