This award provides support for student attendance at the
              upcoming Gordon Research Conference on Nonlinear Optics and Lasers to be held
              at New London, New Hampshire, 25-30 July, 1999.  The conference program covers
              some of the most advanced topics in current ultrafast phenomena, with
              applications from fundamental studies to more applied areas.   Funding for the
              award comes jointly from the Physics Division and the Division of Electrical
              and Communications Systems.
