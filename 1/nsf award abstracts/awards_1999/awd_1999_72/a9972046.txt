Abstract    :
              A recently developed theory for the correlated motion of three charged particles
              will be applied to electron impact ionization of H, electron capture in
              positron-H collisions and ionization in anti-proton atomic hydrogen. The theory
              will be extended to treat the interaction of three neutral particles, a subject
              of importance in cold collisions.
