This award provides support for student attendance at the
              Centennial Meeting of the American Physical Society (APS) in Atlanta, Georgia,

              (DAMOP) will hold its annual meeting as a component of the larger meeting. 
              This award gives students the opportunity to participate in DAMOP sponsored
              events.
