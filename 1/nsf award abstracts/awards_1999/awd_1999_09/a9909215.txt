Abstract    :
              
The award provides travel support for approximately 25 graduate students to
              attend the Eleventh Symposium on Global Change Studies and other coterminous
              scientific conferences that will take place at the American Meteorological
              Society's 80th Annual Meeting, 9-14 January, 2000 in Long Beach CA.  These
              travel scholarships provide a unique opportunity for graduate students to
              deepen understanding of the field, as well as broaden their career
              horizons.
