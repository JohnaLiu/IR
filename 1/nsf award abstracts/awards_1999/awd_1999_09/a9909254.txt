This grant provides partial travel support for participants to
              a conference entitled, "New Vistas in Transatlantic Science and Technology
              Cooperation" held June 21-22, 1999, in Stuttgart, Germany, to promote
              cooperation between the European Union and the United States of America in the
              framework of the EC-US Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement, which was
              launched in October 1998 for an initial five-year period.  It is funded through
              the Office of Multidisciplinary Activities of the Directorate for Mathematical
              and Physical Sciences.
