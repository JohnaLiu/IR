This award provides partial support for the
              featured speakers and for some graduate students to attend the 2000 Spring
              Topology and Dynamics Conference.  The conference is one of a continuing series
              of conferences that are held each spring at a variety of different
              institutions.  This year the conference is being held in San Antonio, Texas,
              and is being hosted by the mathematics departments of the University of the
              Incarnate Word and the University of Texas at San Antonio.  Further information
              on the conference is available at http://universe.uiwtx.edu/~yang/public_site.
