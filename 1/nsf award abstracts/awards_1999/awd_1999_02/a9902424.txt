ABSTRACT

This grant will partially
              cover the cost of the 16th Meeting of the North American Catalysis Society
              (NACS), to be held on May 30 through June 4, 1999, in Boston, Massachusetts. 
              It will finance portion of the travel expenses for young investigators and
              graduate students.  It will also assist financing a poster session and
              organizational expenses.  This NACS meeting will promote high-level discussion
              among national and international colleagues and students, and
              university-industry partnership.
