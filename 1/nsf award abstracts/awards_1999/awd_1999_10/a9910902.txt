Abstract    :
              The objectives of this conference, jointly sponsored by the IEEE Communications
              Society, IEEE Region 8 (Europe, Middle East, Africa), and the Russian Popov
              Society, are to communicate to the most current knowledge about Internet
              technologies and services, and stimulate information exchange and collaboration
              among members of the Internet technical communities Russia, Europe as a whole,
              and North America.  It will be a single-track meeting with speakers both
              invited and responding to an open call.  The approximately 32 presentations
              will be published in a proceedings.  Approximately 120 attendees are expected.
