Abstract    :
              This program will utilize data from the Japanese/U.S. satellite, GEOTAIL, to
              study the magnetic merging process on the dayside magnetopause.  The data will
              be used to demonstrate that inductive electric fields produced by impulsive
              penetration of plasma into the magnetopause play an important role in the
              reconnection process that underlies magnetic merging.
