Abstract    :
              9357888 Oreskes          This young investigator's research focuses on studies
              of mineral processes and industrial society's needs for such minerals.  She
              plans to continue her efforts to advance knowledge about the Earth's crust and
              its contained mineral wealth through her research in geology and geochemistry
              of ore forming systems.  She also plans to continue her efforts to understand
              the processes by which scientific knowledge is formulated through her research
              at Dartmouth College in the history and philosophy of modern earth sciences.
