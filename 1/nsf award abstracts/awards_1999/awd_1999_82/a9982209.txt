This award is to fund the Fifteenth Annual National EPSCoR
              Conference at Perdido  Beach, Orange Beach, Alabama, October 31 to November 3,
              1999.  The theme is "EPSCoR:  Past, Present, and Future."  The conference will
              provide an opportunity for representatives from the 18 EPSCoR states and Puerto
              Rico to get updates on federal programs, to hear invited speakers, and to
              exchange ideas.  It will be attended by approximately 300 persons, including 
              program representatives from the 19 EPSCoR jurisdictions, EPSCoR administrators
              from federal agencies, including the National Science Foundation, members of
              state legislatures, invited speakers and participants, and faculty, staff, and
              students from the EPSCoR jurisdictions.
