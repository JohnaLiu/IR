Abstract    :
              The interaction of ultra-intense lasers with matter is explored using numerical
              simulation.  The project focuses on the dynamics of intense laser-atom
              interactions in the relativistic regime, the negative energy continuum of the
              Dirac equation, the production of electron-positron pairs and pulse propagation
              in dielectric media.
