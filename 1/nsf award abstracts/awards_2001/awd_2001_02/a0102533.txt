Abstract    :
              Virginia State University is submitting this planning proposal to the National
              
Science Foundation Historically Black Colleges and University
              Undergraduate
Program (HBCU-UP) in Mathematics, Engineering, Computer Science,
              and the
Physical Sciences. The Goal of this planning proposal is to conduct an
              assessment 
at VSU on the SMET offerings and develop a plan which will be an
              institutional 
stepping stone toward a National Science Foundation SMET-UP
              five year grant 
proposal with t he long term intention to increase the number
              of well-qualified
minority students graduating in engineering, science and
              mathematics and to 
provide these students with sufficient preparation,
              guidance, and encouragement 
to attend graduate school. 

Key personnel for
              the project will be the Principal Investigator (PI), Co-PI, Activity
              
Coordinator, the director of Institutional Planning and Assessment, the
              director 
of Freshmen, Retention and General Education Programs and all SMET
              department
chairpersons.
