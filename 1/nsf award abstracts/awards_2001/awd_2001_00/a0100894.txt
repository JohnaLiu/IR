This award provides travel support for young investigators to
              attend the 21st International Union of Pure and Applied Physics Triennial
              Conference on Thermodynamics and Statistical Physics (Statphys-21) to be held
              in Cancun, Mexico, on July 15-20, 2001.  The award is co-funded by the
              Divisions of Materials Research, Mathematical Sciences, Chemistry, Physics and
              the Office of Multidisciplinary Activities in MPS.  The International Division
              is also providing support.
