This grant supports a planning visit to Hong Kong by Dr.
              Alexander Matthews, Kansas State University.  Dr. Matthews plans to carry out a
              series of discussions with senior researchers at the Hong Kong University of
              Science and Technology to develop research protocols for a multi-year program
              of cooperative research and exchange of students in the area of environmental
              quality control.
