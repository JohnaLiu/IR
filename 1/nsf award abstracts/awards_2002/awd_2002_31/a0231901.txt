Abstract    :
              The is a request on behalf of the Carbon Cycle Interagency Working Group to
              continue funding for scientific coordination activities for the carbon cycle
              interagency initiative in FY2002.  These activities will include two meetings
              (April & December 2002) of the Carbon Cycle Scientific Steering Group, chaired
              by Chris Field, sponsorship of three workshops (an Integrated Assessment
              Modeling Workshop, a Data Assimilation Workshop, and a Methane Workshop),
              support for an Interim International  Carbon Project, and support for a half
              time person to serve at the Carbon Cycle Program Office.
