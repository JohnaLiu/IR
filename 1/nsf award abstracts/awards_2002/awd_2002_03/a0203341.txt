Abstract    :
               

This award provides funding for a three-year Combined Research-Curriculum
              Development (CRCD) program, Seamless Integration of Information Devices: A
              Focus on Emerging Technologies in New Product Development," at the University
              of Pittsburgh, under the direction of Dr. Michael Lovell. The overall objective
              of this program is to develop an integrated and innovative curriculum, to
              enhance the product design and entrepreneurial skills of graduates, that
              incorporates a multi-disciplinary research program across the School of
              Engineering and into the Katz School of Business.
