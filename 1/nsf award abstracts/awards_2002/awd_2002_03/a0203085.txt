Developing a Basic Research
              Program for Digital Government

This workshop grant is intended to develop a
              long-term, basic research agenda for Digital Government research in the areas
              of social and political sciences.  Attendees will include Computer/Information
              scientists, social scientists, and government executives.  The workshop will be
              developed and managed by Harvard's John F. Kennedy School of government.
