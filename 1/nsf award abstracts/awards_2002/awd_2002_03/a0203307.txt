Abstract    :
               

This award provides funding for a three-year,  Combined Research-Curriculum
              Development (CRCD) program, entitled "Collaborative Case-Based Learning in
              Engineering Ethics," at the University of Pittsburgh, under the direction of
              Dr. Kevin D. Ashley. The overall objective of this project is to design, build,
              field and evaluate the COM-PETE (Collaborative On-line Methodology for
              Professional Ethics Tutoring Environment) program which will support students
              in collaboratively analyzing and deliberating about professional ethics
              problems.
