Abstract    :
              A tomographic model that includes Axial volcano and nearby portions of the Juan
              de Fuca Ridge will be used to develop quantitative 3D models of stress and
              deformation in this region.  Predictions of these models will be compared with
              geodetic, tidal-loading, and seismicity data.
