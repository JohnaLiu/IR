Abstract    :
              An Industry/University Fellowship award will assist with the conduct of
              collaborative research at the Laboratory for Telecommunications Research in
              Telcordia Technologies.  Telcordia is an industrial affiliate of the NSF
              Industry/University Cooperative Research Center for Software Engineering
              Research (SERC).  The PI is currently  a professor at Purdue and a former site
              director of SERC.  The general are of research deals with reliable operation of
              next generation systems.  A next generation system is a collection of
              distributed components intended to work in harmony with the objective of
              providing services  to authorized customers in an efficient, secure, safe, and
              reliable manner.
