Abstract    :
              This award provides funding for the support of a three year continuing Research
              Experiences for Teachers (RET) Site program entitled, "Enrichment Experiences
              in Engineering (ES3) for Teachers Summer Research Program," at Texas A&M
              University, under the direction of Dr. Karen L. Butler-Purry.  This program
              will involve a minimum of 15 high school math and/or science teachers annually
              from Texas Urban System Initiative, Rural Systemic Initiative, and the
              Comprehensive Partnership for Mathematics and Science Achievement schools in
              research projects in the College of Engineering.
